About

The Library is located on the second floor and mezzanine level of Beatty Hall at the center of the Wentworth Institute of Technology campus. We partner with the WIT community in teaching, learning, and scholarship by providing resources and services that anticipate and respond to changing information needs. Make the Library your destination of choice for your information, research, and study needs. The Library has the people, resources, services, and tools to ensure your success.
